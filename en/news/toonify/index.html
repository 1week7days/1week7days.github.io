Toonify | A web based AI tool that can transfer your photos into Disney characters.

· 2021-05-01 · Sunny · # onlineTool

Toonify was developed by Justin Pinkney and Doron Adler, and it is a series of transformation tools. Easy to use, and all you need is a selfie.

Introduction

Before we dive in, let's see some examples.

For more examples, please head to Toonify

Upon the article was published，there are five tools for Toonify. See below:

○ [Free] Toonify Classic：The original Toonify.
○ [HD] Toonify HD：Beter quality, higher resolution.
○ [SD] Caricature：A caricature portrait of yourself.
○ [SD] Emojify：Make yourself an emoji.
○ [SD] Halloweenify：Make yourself scary.

Toonify uses a depp learning method called Generative Adversarial Networks (GAN). To use the tool, all you need is a selfie of yourself or a person.

*Note：the website does not store any or use your uploaded images for anything other than producing your Toonification. Your uploaded image is discarded as soon as you get your result.

Here is an image from thispersondoesnotexist（all the website can do is to generate a random selfie of a person who does not exist in the world. Make sure you cange the extension from .jfif to .jpeg before you use it for toonification if you want to test Toonify.)

How to use： Select your transformation >> Browse >> click Toonify >> Download & save
